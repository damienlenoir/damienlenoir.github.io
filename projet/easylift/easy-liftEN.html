Easy-lift project

When I was living in ireland, I realized that people don't do carpooling so often. Buses are expensive and not very reliable.


After I get graduated in software development, I thought this would be a nice opportunity to practive my computing skills. Of course I never wanted to copy the well known "blablacar", or to reinvente the wheel. I just wanted to create something on my own, with a nice user interface and easy to use.


You can visit the website here: http://easy-lift-carpooling.com/ And view the source dode there: https://github.com/damienlenoir/easy-lift-carpooling
